ABOUT ASPERITAS

in a world that’s mostly empty nowadays, things can get lonely - and weird - fast. luckily, as captain you and your crew take care of each other on the migrant, an airship that drifts around to the magical islands that crowd the skies. that is, at least until you pick up piper, an angel who’s not sure of where to go, and his overprotective sister peregrine, and realize there might be a way to get back the way the world truly used to be - as long as you’re up for a little bit of danger, battle, and revealing the mysteries in asperitas.
